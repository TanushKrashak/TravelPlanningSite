Italy Travel Package

Experience the charm of Italy with its art, culture, and cuisine in this unforgettable 7-day adventure.

Package Overview

This all-inclusive 7-day package takes you through the historical and romantic cities of Rome, Florence, Venice, and the Amalfi Coast.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Rome

Arrive in Rome and check-in at your hotel. Enjoy a guided evening tour of the Colosseum and Roman Forum, followed by a delicious Italian dinner.

Day 2: Rome's Iconic Landmarks

Explore the Vatican Museums, Sistine Chapel, and St. Peter's Basilica. In the afternoon, take a stroll through Piazza Navona and the Pantheon.

Day 3: Florence - Art and Culture

Travel to Florence, Italy’s art capital. Visit the Uffizi Gallery and Michelangelo’s David. Enjoy an evening walk along the Arno River and Ponte Vecchio.

Day 4: Florence - Wine and Tuscan Countryside

Take a day trip to the Tuscan countryside for a wine tasting tour, where you will sample wines and local specialties in a charming vineyard.

Day 5: Venice - Canals and Romance

Travel to Venice, where you’ll enjoy a gondola ride through its famous canals. Visit St. Mark's Square and the Rialto Bridge.

Day 6: Amalfi Coast

Travel to the stunning Amalfi Coast. Enjoy a day of relaxation and sightseeing in towns like Positano and Ravello, famous for their breathtaking views and beautiful beaches.

Day 7: Departure

Enjoy a relaxing breakfast by the coast before your departure. Take a leisurely stroll along the seaside and head to the airport for your flight home.
