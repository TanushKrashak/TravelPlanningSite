Boston Travel Package

Step into the heart of American history with a trip to Boston, home to revolutionary landmarks and vibrant culture.

Package Overview

Spend 5 days exploring the historical sites of Boston, from the Freedom Trail to the Boston Tea Party Ships and Museum. Experience rich culture, vibrant neighborhoods, and New England charm.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Boston

Arrive in Boston and transfer to your hotel. Enjoy a walk through the historic Beacon Hill neighborhood in the evening.

Day 2: Freedom Trail

Explore the iconic Freedom Trail, visiting landmarks such as Boston Common, the Massachusetts State House, and Paul Revere's House.

Day 3: Harvard University & MIT

Take a tour of the prestigious Harvard University in Cambridge and visit the cutting-edge Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT).

Day 4: Boston Tea Party Museum

Experience the Boston Tea Party at the interactive museum. Later, stroll along the waterfront and enjoy a seafood dinner at the famous Quincy Market.

Day 5: Departure

Enjoy a leisurely breakfast before heading to the airport for your flight back home.
