Bora Bora Travel Package

Escape to paradise in Bora Bora, where crystal-clear lagoons and luxurious resorts await you!

Package Overview

This 7-day luxury escape to Bora Bora offers the perfect combination of relaxation, adventure, and romance. Snorkel in pristine waters, stay in overwater bungalows, and indulge in spa treatments.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Bora Bora

Arrive at Bora Bora Airport and transfer to your luxurious overwater bungalow. Enjoy a welcome cocktail and relax by the crystal-clear lagoon.

Day 2: Lagoon Tour & Snorkeling

Embark on a guided lagoon tour, visiting coral gardens and snorkeling with vibrant marine life. Enjoy a beachside lunch.

Day 3: Matira Beach & Lagoonarium

Relax at Matira Beach and explore the shallow waters of Lagoonarium, where you can swim with stingrays and sharks.

Day 6: Spa & Relaxation

Indulge in a day of pampering with a luxurious spa treatment at your resort. Relax and unwind by the lagoon.

Day 7: Departure

After breakfast, enjoy some final moments on the beach before transferring to the airport for your return flight.
