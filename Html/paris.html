Paris Travel Package

Experience the romance, history, and culture of Paris, from the Eiffel Tower to the Seine River!

Package Overview

This 7-day package offers a blend of history, culture, and culinary delights in Paris. Visit iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame, and enjoy gourmet dining experiences.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Paris

Arrive at Charles de Gaulle Airport. Transfer to your hotel and rest. Evening at leisure to explore the city lights.

Day 2: Eiffel Tower & Seine River Cruise

Start your day with a visit to the Eiffel Tower. Enjoy panoramic views, followed by a Seine River cruise to see Paris from the water.

Day 3: Louvre Museum & Montmartre

Tour the Louvre Museum to see iconic works of art like the Mona Lisa. Later, head to Montmartre and explore the charming streets and Sacré-Cœur Basilica.

Day 4: Notre-Dame & Sainte-Chapelle

Visit the iconic Notre-Dame Cathedral and explore the beautiful Sainte-Chapelle with its stunning stained glass windows.

Day 5: Versailles Palace

Take a day trip to the opulent Palace of Versailles, exploring the grand gardens and the Hall of Mirrors.

Day 6: Champs-Élysées & Arc de Triomphe

Stroll along the Champs-Élysées, shop at luxury boutiques, and visit the Arc de Triomphe for a magnificent view of the city.

Day 7: Departure

After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your flight back home, with unforgettable memories of Paris!
