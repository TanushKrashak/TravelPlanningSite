Bali Travel Package

Your perfect tropical getaway to Bali awaits. Relax, explore, and experience Bali like never before!

Package Overview

This all-inclusive package offers 7 days of adventure and relaxation in Bali. Explore breathtaking beaches, ancient temples, lush jungles, and vibrant culture. We've created a perfect itinerary for you!

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Bali

Arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport, Bali. Transfer to your hotel for check-in and relax. Enjoy a traditional Balinese welcome dinner.

Day 2: Uluwatu Temple & Kecak Dance

Explore the famous Uluwatu Temple perched on a cliff. Watch the mesmerizing Kecak dance performance at sunset. Enjoy dinner at Jimbaran Bay.

Day 3: Beach Day in Seminyak

Spend a relaxing day at Seminyak Beach. Try water sports such as surfing, parasailing, or simply enjoy the beachside cafes. Evening at leisure for shopping or dining.

Day 4: Sacred Monkey Forest & Ubud Village

Visit the Sacred Monkey Forest in Ubud. Explore the art markets, visit rice terraces, and enjoy a scenic lunch. In the evening, unwind with a Balinese massage.

Day 5: Mount Batur Sunrise Trek

Early morning trek to Mount Batur for a stunning sunrise view. After the trek, relax in the hot springs nearby before returning to the hotel for rest.

Day 6: Explore Local Markets in Ubud

Visit the Ubud Art Market, where you can shop for handmade crafts and souvenirs. Afternoon visit to the Tegenungan Waterfall and enjoy the natural surroundings.

Day 7: Departure

Enjoy a final breakfast in Bali before transferring to the airport for your flight back home. Carry wonderful memories of your Bali adventure!
