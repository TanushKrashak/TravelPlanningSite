Finland Travel Package

Explore the magical Northern Lights, pristine landscapes, and charming cities of Finland.

Package Overview

Discover Finland's natural beauty and rich culture in this 7-day adventure, from Helsinki's design scene to Lapland's arctic wonders.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Helsinki

Arrive in Helsinki, Finland's vibrant capital. Explore the Senate Square, Helsinki Cathedral, and enjoy a walk along the seaside promenade.

Day 2: Helsinki City Tour

Visit Suomenlinna Fortress, the National Museum of Finland, and the famous Temppeliaukio Rock Church.

Day 3: Travel to Rovaniemi

Fly to Rovaniemi, the capital of Lapland. Visit Santa Claus Village and cross the Arctic Circle.

Day 4: Explore Lapland

Enjoy a thrilling snowmobile safari and a reindeer sled ride. Experience the magical Northern Lights in the evening.

Day 5: Visit Kemi SnowCastle

Visit the Kemi SnowCastle, where you can admire ice sculptures and enjoy a meal at the ice restaurant.

Day 6: Santa’s Secret Workshop

Visit Santa’s Secret Workshop for some Christmas cheer. Enjoy a traditional Finnish sauna experience in the evening.

Day 7: Departure

Return to Helsinki and enjoy your last day before catching your flight home.
