Switzerland Travel Package

Embark on a journey to the majestic Swiss Alps, explore charming villages, and indulge in Swiss culture and cuisine.

Package Overview

This all-inclusive package offers 7 days of breathtaking beauty in Switzerland. From snow-capped mountains to picturesque lakes and charming towns, experience the best of the Swiss Alps!

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Zurich

Arrive in Zurich, Switzerland. Check-in at your hotel and enjoy a relaxing evening exploring Zurich's old town and waterfront.

Day 2: Lucerne and Mount Pilatus

Take a scenic train ride to Lucerne and then a cable car to the top of Mount Pilatus for stunning views of the Alps. Explore the charming town of Lucerne in the afternoon.

Day 3: Interlaken and Lauterbrunnen

Head to Interlaken and Lauterbrunnen. Enjoy the views of waterfalls and the famous Jungfrau region. You can opt for a visit to Jungfraujoch - the top of Europe.

Day 4: Zermatt and the Matterhorn

Travel to Zermatt, a car-free mountain village at the base of the iconic Matterhorn. Spend the day exploring the area or take a cable car ride to Gornergrat for panoramic mountain views.

Day 5: Montreux and Lake Geneva

Travel to Montreux, located on the shores of Lake Geneva. Explore the beautiful vineyards, take a boat ride on the lake, and visit the iconic Chillon Castle.

Day 6: Bern - The Capital of Switzerland

Visit Bern, the medieval capital of Switzerland. Explore the historic old town, the Zytglogge clock tower, and the famous Bear Park.

Day 7: Departure

Enjoy a farewell breakfast in Switzerland before heading to the airport for your return journey home, filled with unforgettable memories of the Swiss Alps.
