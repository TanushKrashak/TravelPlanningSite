Dubai Travel Package

Your luxurious escape to Dubai awaits. Explore modern marvels, shopping, and desert adventures like never before!

Package Overview

This all-inclusive package offers 7 days of exciting exploration in Dubai. From stunning skyscrapers to desert safaris, we’ve curated the perfect itinerary!

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Dubai

Arrive at Dubai International Airport, check-in to your hotel, and spend the evening at leisure exploring the Dubai Mall and Fountain Show.

Day 2: Burj Khalifa & Dubai Mall

Visit the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world, and enjoy panoramic views of the city. Later, explore the luxury shopping and dining at the Dubai Mall.

Day 3: Desert Safari Adventure

Experience the thrill of a desert safari. Enjoy dune bashing, camel rides, and a traditional Arabian BBQ dinner under the stars.

Day 4: Atlantis The Palm & Aquaventure Waterpark

Spend a fun-filled day at Atlantis The Palm, exploring the hotel’s luxurious amenities, and visit the Aquaventure Waterpark for water slides and marine experiences.

Day 5: Dubai Marina & Jumeirah Beach

Stroll along the Dubai Marina, explore the luxury yachts, and enjoy some leisure time at the pristine Jumeirah Beach.

Day 6: Cultural Dubai & Shopping

Visit the Dubai Museum and the Al Fahidi Historical Neighborhood to experience the city’s culture and history. Enjoy shopping at the Gold and Spice Souks.

Day 7: Departure

Enjoy a relaxing breakfast at your hotel. Spend your last few hours shopping or visiting local attractions before heading to the airport for your flight back home.
