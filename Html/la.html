Los Angeles Travel Package

Explore the vibrant city of Los Angeles, from Hollywood glamour to beautiful beaches!

Package Overview

This exciting 7-day adventure takes you through the best of Los Angeles, including Hollywood, Santa Monica, Venice Beach, and more. A perfect combination of entertainment, culture, and relaxation.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Los Angeles

Arrive at LAX Airport, Los Angeles. Transfer to your hotel and unwind. Evening at leisure, with optional dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 2: Hollywood Tour

Tour the Hollywood Walk of Fame, see the TCL Chinese Theatre, and visit the iconic Hollywood Sign. Enjoy lunch at a local café.

Day 3: Venice Beach & Santa Monica Pier

Spend a day exploring Venice Beach's boardwalk, then head to Santa Monica Pier for a relaxing evening by the ocean.

Day 4: Beverly Hills & Rodeo Drive

Explore the upscale neighborhood of Beverly Hills. Walk along Rodeo Drive and shop in the world's most glamorous retail district.

Day 5: Griffith Observatory & Sunset Blvd

Visit Griffith Observatory for breathtaking views of the city. Drive down Sunset Blvd and experience LA’s legendary nightlife.

Day 6: Malibu & Pacific Coast Highway

Take a scenic drive along the Pacific Coast Highway to Malibu. Relax on the sandy beaches and enjoy lunch with ocean views.

Day 7: Departure

After breakfast, check out and transfer to LAX for your return flight home, with memories of your LA adventure!
